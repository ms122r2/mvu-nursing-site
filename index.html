FAQ's

Are Maryville's nursing programs accredited?

Yes. All of Maryville's nursing programs are accredited by the Commission on Collegiate Nursing Education (CCNE), the gold standard in nursing education accreditation.

Is coursework really 100% online?

Yes. All didactic coursework is completed online. MSN and DNP programs include clinical components that you complete locally in your own community with placement support from Maryville.

Do I need to take the GRE?

No. Maryville does not require GRE or entrance exams for any online nursing program.

How long does each program take?

RN to BSN: 12 months. Accelerated BSN: 16 months. MSN-FNP: 24 months. DNP: 24-36 months. All timelines assume full-time enrollment.

What financial aid is available?

Maryville offers scholarships, employer tuition reimbursement partnerships, federal financial aid (FAFSA), and military benefits. Contact an enrollment advisor for a personalized financial plan.
